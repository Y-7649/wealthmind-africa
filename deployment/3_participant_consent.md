# Participant Information & Consent

*Plain-language consent for the WealthMind Africa Financial Behaviour Assessment. This mirrors the in-app consent screen and can be shown or read aloud before a session.*

---

## What this is
WealthMind Africa is a student-built research project studying **how people of different ages and life stages make everyday financial decisions** in Kenya. You'll answer about 8 short questions and immediately see your own financial-behaviour scores.

## It takes about 2 minutes.

## What we collect
- Your **answers** to the questions (income range, saving, spending habits) and optional **age band, life stage, and gender** for grouping.
- **Nothing that identifies you** — no name, no email, no phone number, no account.

## How it's used
- Only **anonymous, aggregate** patterns are studied and reported (for example, "students showed more present bias than working adults"). Groups smaller than 8 are never reported.
- Aggregate findings may appear in the public Impact Report and in the creator's university applications.

## Your choices
- Taking part is **completely voluntary**.
- At the end you choose: **"Yes, include my anonymous responses"** or **"No, show my results only."**
- If you choose **No**, you still see your full results and **nothing is saved**.
- There are no right or wrong answers and no consequences either way.

## If you are under 18
Please take part only if you're comfortable doing so. Because the assessment is fully anonymous and collects no identifying information, no personal data about you is stored. Ask a parent, guardian, or teacher if you have any questions first.
